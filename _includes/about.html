About

Data Jujitsu is a data science consultancy providing expert solutions, training and advice to international clients who want to reveal the power of their data. It was founded by David Springate in 2017.

  • Expertise

    David completed a PhD in Evolutionary and Computational Biology at Manchester University in 2012. He worked for more than three years as an academic researcher in machine learning and as a medical statistician.

  • Industry Experience

    David spent 18 months as lead data scientist at a big data technology company in Manchester. He developed a data fusion platform, designed and implemented big data analytics systems in production using Spark, R and Python and presented to clients from large multinational corporations

  • Data Science Community

    David has been an active member of the Data Science community since 2010, blogging, presenting at conferences and meetups, reviewing data science books and contributing to online discussions.

  • Consulting

    Since setting up Data Jujitsu, David has written Microsoft's "Analyzing Big Data with Microsoft R" online course, implemented a marketing analytics platform for a multinational ecommerce company, and performed several bespoke modelling and text mining analysis projects

  • Industry sectors

    David has worked across academia, biotech, digital media, market research and public health. He is particularly interested in ehealth, proptech, smart cities, agritech, edtech, charities and digital media.
