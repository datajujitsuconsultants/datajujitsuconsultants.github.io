Data Fusion

Data fusion is the process of integrating multiple datasets into a single fused dataset. This enables them to be analysed together in a consistent way that is more useful than analysing the original datasets independently.

We have extensive experience in conducting large survey and media data fusions using the MRI, Simmons and Numeris establishment surveys to create powerful media planning tools.

We are experts at implementing:

Unconstrained Fusions: Where one dataset is calibrated to another from a possibly more trusted source

Constrained Fusion: Where two well-calibrated datasets are combined by sharing the weights from one to the other. This leaves both datasets essentially unchanged
